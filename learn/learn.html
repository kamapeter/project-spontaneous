Introducing LEARN

The following highlights the structure of our LEARN Lessons:
  • MODULES: This are short structured Lessons that focus on one aspect of the topic been discussed
  • PHYSICS IN REAL LIFE: They show how physical principles and concepts applies in our universe. The usually appear at the end of each module.
  • RECHARGE: These are short multiple-choice questions that appear at the end of each module.
  • QUIZ: These feature is designed to assess the students understanding of the topic discussed so far. Recommendations are offered at the end of each test when necessary.
